At-A-Change, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to Life Literacy education - empowering citizens with knowledge of their everyday legal rights to build confidence and create positive change. Founded on principles of pure infinite love, we believe educated citizens serve our greatest and highest good.

Our Mission

At-A-Change, Inc. serves as a comprehensive resource hub for Life Literacy education, focusing on empowering citizens with knowledge of their everyday legal rights.

We enable individuals to navigate our world with confidence, rooted in the belief that educated citizens serve our greatest and highest good. Everything we do is founded on principles of pure infinite love.
